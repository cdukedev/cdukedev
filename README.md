### 🌱 Tech for Good, *my north star*

I was a community builder before I was an engineer (gardens, sustainability, small business, a music school), and I went and got the most powerful tool available so I could build the things I'd always dreamed of. As the cost of building collapses, I want to put that power in the hands of the people and communities who get left out.

- ⭐ **WayWright** *(Founder · my centerpiece)*: a program that closes the gap between **learning** and **shipping for impact**: it pairs emerging builders with **industry professionals** *and* a **real nonprofit/organization partner**, then runs an **Agile SDLC** so they deliver **production-ready** software the organization actually depends on. Democratizing who gets to build, and pointing it at the missions that matter.
- 🌻 **FlorShare**: mobile app (native iOS + web) for building **backyard gardens** and **supporting local nurseries** (food access, sustainability, local economies).
- 🥗 **Gathering MIA**: a **food-delivery network** linking **food banks** to **homebound neighbors in food deserts**.
- 🤝 *FlorShare & Gathering MIA are the proof, and the case for WayWright: solo mission builds that, run through WayWright's model, become production-grade rollouts.*
- 🎓 **Mentorship & access** *(what I most want to be known for)*: paying forward the people who opened doors for me: full-stack mentor (BrainStation), tech-workshop lead (President, INIT @ MDC), and Green Leaders apprentice & volunteer engineer at Friends of The Underline.
